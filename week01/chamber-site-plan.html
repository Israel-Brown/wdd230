Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Welcome to the site plan for the Kingston Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

Kingston Chamber of Commerce

The site name was selected because it represents Kingston, the capital city, and serves as the hub for business activities and services in the region.

Site Purpose

  • To provide local businesses with resources to grow, network, and succeed.
  • To inform and connect the community with economic development programs, local events, and public services.

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: Blue (#007BFF) - Used for headings, links, and call-to-action buttons
  • Secondary Color: Light Gray (#F4F4F4) - Used for background sections and content containers
  • Accent Color: Dark Gray (#333333) - Used for text, borders, and footers

Typography

  • Font 1: "Roboto" - Used for headings, navigation, and large text.
  • Font 2: "Open Sans" - Used for body text, descriptions, and content.

Wireframe

Mobile View: Single column layout with stacked navigation, hero section, main content areas (Events, Membership), footer.

Desktop View: Two-column layout with a navigation sidebar on the left and content on the right, featured events at the top, footer at the bottom.
